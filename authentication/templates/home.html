Who Are We

"Insights on Hockey League Team Analytics" is a comprehensive project aimed at providing hockey teams with advanced tools and analytics to optimize their performance. Through modules like Match Analysis, Prediction, Visualization, Standings, and Injury Tracking, teams can gain valuable insights into player performance, game strategies, and overall team dynamics. By leveraging data-driven approaches, teams can make informed decisions, enhance player development, and improve their competitive edge in the dynamic landscape of hockey leagues.
